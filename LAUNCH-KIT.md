# 🚀 Launch Kit — copy, paste, done

Traffic is the only thing standing between this site and real income. This kit
removes the thinking: copy a block, paste it where indicated, done. None of it
requires writing anything yourself.

> Replace `YOUR-SITE-URL` with your real Netlify URL everywhere below.

---

## 1) Turn on income (pick the fastest for you)

**Fastest, no approval — a tip button (live the same day):**
1. Go to <https://ko-fi.com> → sign up (free) → it gives you a page like
   `https://ko-fi.com/yourname`.
2. Open `assets/js/config.js`, set `SUPPORT_URL: "https://ko-fi.com/yourname"`,
   commit. A "Support this site" button now appears on every page.

**Most passive, slower — display ads:**
1. <https://adsense.google.com> → add your site → get your `ca-pub-…` ID.
2. Paste it into `ADSENSE_PUBLISHER_ID` in `config.js` and into `ads.txt`.

You can do both. Either way it's one paste per option.

---

## 2) Get found on Google (10 min, the highest-impact free step)

1. Go to <https://search.google.com/search-console>.
2. Add your site (Netlify makes domain verification easy).
3. Submit your sitemap: enter `sitemap.xml` and click submit.

That's it — Google will start indexing all 14 tools and the guides. This is the
single biggest lever on long-term traffic and costs nothing.
