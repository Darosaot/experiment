About QuickTools

QuickTools is a growing collection of free online utilities — calculators, generators and converters — built around one idea: the tool should be the page. No sign-ups, no paywalls, no uploads, no clutter. You arrive, you do the thing, you leave.

Private by default

Every tool runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Whatever you type — passwords, documents, financial figures — stays on your device and is never sent to a server. You can read more in our privacy policy.

How it stays free

The site is supported by unobtrusive advertising. That revenue covers hosting and lets us keep every tool free for everyone, with no premium tiers or locked features.

Suggestions

Want a tool we don't have yet? We're always adding more. The most-requested utilities get built first.
