About this QR code generator

QR ("Quick Response") codes let anyone open a link, see a message, or save a contact by pointing their phone camera at a square. This generator creates them entirely in your browser — your data is never uploaded — and lets you download a high-resolution PNG with no watermark and no usage limits.

What can you encode?

  • A website URL — the most common use, perfect for posters, menus and business cards.
  • Plain text — a message, a Wi-Fi name, a coupon code.
  • Contact info or email — paste a mailto: or tel: link.

FAQ

Do these QR codes expire?

No. The code is "static" — it encodes your text directly, so it works forever and has no tracking or expiry.

Can I use it commercially?

Yes. The PNG you download is yours to use on flyers, packaging, products or anywhere else, free of charge.

Why keep good contrast?

Scanners need clear contrast between foreground and background. Dark code on a light background scans most reliably.
