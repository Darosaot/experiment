About Lorem Ipsum

Lorem Ipsum is scrambled Latin-like placeholder text that designers and developers have used since the 1500s to fill layouts without the distraction of real, readable copy. It lets you focus on the visual design — spacing, type, hierarchy — before the final content is ready.

FAQ

Why not just type "asdf asdf"?

Lorem Ipsum has a roughly natural distribution of word and sentence lengths, so layouts look realistic — repeated letters don't.

Is it always the same text?

It's randomly assembled from the classic Lorem Ipsum word list each time, so regenerating gives fresh filler.
