About this age calculator

This tool calculates the precise time between your date of birth and today (or any date you choose), accounting for the varying length of months and for leap years. It's handy for forms that ask for an exact age, for milestone celebrations, or just curiosity.

FAQ

How is age calculated?

We count complete years first, then the remaining whole months, then the leftover days — the same way you'd say "32 years, 4 months and 12 days."

Does it handle leap years?

Yes. Date math uses your browser's calendar engine, which correctly handles leap days and month lengths.
