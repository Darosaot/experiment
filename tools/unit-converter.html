About this unit converter

From cooking and DIY to travel and schoolwork, converting between units is a daily task. This tool handles the four most-needed categories — length, weight, temperature and digital storage — with accurate conversion factors, all calculated instantly in your browser.

Common conversions

  • Kilometres ↔ miles for travel and running distances.
  • Kilograms ↔ pounds for weight, shipping and fitness.
  • Celsius ↔ Fahrenheit for weather and cooking.
  • MB ↔ GB for files, storage and data plans.

FAQ

How accurate is it?

It uses standard SI conversion factors and full floating-point precision, then displays a sensible number of decimals.

Does it work offline?

Yes, once the page has loaded it runs entirely in your browser with no further network calls.
