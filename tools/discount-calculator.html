About this discount calculator

Sale signs can be confusing — especially "25% off, then an extra 10% at the till." This calculator does the maths for you, including stacked discounts, which are applied one after another rather than simply added together.

Why two 25% discounts isn't 50% off

Stacked discounts apply to the already-reduced price. A 25% discount followed by another 25% leaves you paying 0.75 × 0.75 = 56.25% of the original — a 43.75% effective discount, not 50%.

FAQ

Can I use one discount only?

Yes — just leave the "extra discount" field at 0.
