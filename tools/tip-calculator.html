About this tip calculator

No more awkward mental maths at the end of a meal. Enter the bill, pick a tip percentage, and optionally split between any number of people — the tool shows the tip, the grand total and exactly what each person owes.

How much should you tip?

  • 15% — standard for adequate service in many countries.
  • 18–20% — good to excellent service.
  • In much of Europe, service is often included — check the bill first.

FAQ

Is the tip calculated before or after tax?

This tool tips on the amount you enter. If you prefer to tip on the pre-tax subtotal, enter that figure as the bill.
