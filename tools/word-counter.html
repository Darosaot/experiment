About this word counter

Whether you're writing a 500-word essay, staying under a 280-character tweet limit, or hitting a freelance article target, this free word counter gives you live feedback as you type. It counts words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, paragraphs, and estimates reading time based on an average reading speed of 200 words per minute.

How to use it

  1. Click into the text box and type, or paste text you've already written.
  2. Watch the counts update instantly — there's no "calculate" button.
  3. Use Copy text to grab your content, or Clear to start over.

Is my text private?

Yes. This tool runs entirely in your browser. Your text is never sent to a server, stored, or logged. You can even disconnect from the internet and it will keep working.

FAQ

How is a "word" counted?

A word is any sequence of characters separated by spaces or line breaks. Multiple spaces are ignored, so accidental double-spaces won't inflate your count.

How is reading time calculated?

We divide your word count by 200 words per minute, the average adult silent reading speed. It's an estimate to help you gauge length.

Is there a character or word limit?

No. You can paste very large documents and the counter keeps up. Performance depends only on your device.
