**Stop guessing whether your freelance business is actually making money.**

This is one clean spreadsheet that runs the entire money side of your solo
business — and it does the math for you.

- 💶 Income received vs. still outstanding

1. **Start Here** — a 60-second guide

3. **Income & Invoices** — track Paid / Unpaid / Overdue
4. **Expenses** — categorised, with a deductible flag for easy tax time
5. **Rate Calculator** — the exact hourly & day rate you must charge to hit
   your income goal
6. **Projects** — your pipeline + your *real* effective hourly rate
7. **Settings** — set it up once in two minutes

**Why freelancers love it**
- No formulas to build — it's all done. Just type.
