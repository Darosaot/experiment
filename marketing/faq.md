# FAQ (paste into your Etsy/Gumroad listing or use to answer buyers)

**Q: Do I need Excel?**
No. The file opens in Microsoft Excel, Google Sheets (free), Apple Numbers and
LibreOffice. For Google Sheets, upload it to Google Drive and choose *Open with
▸ Google Sheets*.

**Q: Is this a subscription?**
No — one-time purchase, yours forever.

**Q: Do I need to know spreadsheet formulas?**
No. Everything is built in. You only type your invoices and expenses; the math
and charts happen automatically.

**Q: Can I customise it?**
Yes — edit the categories, statuses, colours and labels freely. The Settings tab
controls most of it.

**Q: Will it calculate my exact tax?**
It gives a planning *estimate* based on the set-aside % you choose. It's not a
substitute for an accountant or local tax rules.

**Q: How do I get the file?**
Instant digital download right after purchase. Nothing is shipped.

**Q: What currency does it use?**
Any — set your currency symbol on the Settings tab.

**Q: I'm having trouble opening it.**
Message me / reply to your receipt and I'll help right away.
