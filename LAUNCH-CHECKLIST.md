# 🚀 Your launch checklist (the only part I can't legally do for you)

I built the entire product, the images, and all the sales copy. The only steps
left are the ones that legally require **your** identity and bank details (KYC) —
no one can do these for you. Budget about **20 minutes, once.**

## The fastest path (Etsy — recommended)

1. **Create a free Etsy account** → https://www.etsy.com/sell
   (~5 min; you'll verify email + add a bank/PayPal for payouts — this is the
   KYC step only you can do).
2. **Open a shop.** Pick any shop name. Set currency to EUR.
3. **Create a new listing** → choose **"Digital"**.
4. **Upload the product file:** `product/Freelancer-Finance-Toolkit.xlsx`
   *(optionally also `product/BUYER-QUICKSTART.md`).*
5. **Upload the 3 images** from `product/listing-images/` (1-cover first).
6. **Paste the title, tags and description** from `marketing/etsy-listing.md`.
7. **Set the price** to €11.99 and **publish**. (Etsy charges €0.18 to list.)
8. **Turn on auto-renew** so it relists after each sale.

✅ That's it — you now have a live product that can sell while you do nothing.

## Optional second channel (Gumroad — 10 min, free to list)
1. Sign up → https://gumroad.com (connect bank/PayPal — KYC again).
2. New product → upload the same `.xlsx` + quick-start.
3. Paste everything from `marketing/gumroad-listing.md`, price €12, publish.
4. Use this link in any social posts / your bio.

## Then (optional, to actually hit €150/month)
Open `marketing/promotion-free-traffic.md`. The near-passive must-do is just:
**use all 13 Etsy tags + ask buyers for a review.** Pinterest pins (one
afternoon) are the best free booster.
