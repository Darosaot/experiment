# Freelancer Finance & Business Toolkit — Quick Start

Thank you for your purchase! You'll be up and running in about 5 minutes.

## What you got
- **Freelancer-Finance-Toolkit.xlsx** — one file, 7 tabs, fully automatic.

## Set up (once)
1. Open the file in **Excel**, or upload it to **Google Drive** and choose
   *File ▸ Save as Google Sheets* (or *Open with ▸ Google Sheets*).
2. Go to the **Settings** tab. Fill in:
   - Your currency symbol (€, $, £…)
   - Your annual income goal
   - Your realistic billable hours per year
   - Your tax set-aside % (a safe rough default is filled in)
3. That's it — everything else updates itself.

## Daily / weekly use
- **Income tab** — add one row per invoice. Set the status to *Paid* when the
  money lands. Only *Paid* rows count as real income on your Dashboard.
- **Expenses tab** — add costs, pick a category, mark *Yes* if it's deductible.
- **Dashboard tab** — read your profit, tax to set aside, and take-home. Charts
  update automatically.
- **Rate Calculator tab** — tells you the minimum hourly and day rate you must
  charge to hit your goal.
- **Projects tab** — track your pipeline and your *real* effective hourly rate.

## Tips
- **Dates:** type them as `2026-01-31` (year-month-day) so the monthly charts
  work correctly.
- **More rows:** copy any formatted row downward — the formulas extend by
  themselves. There's room for 250 entries per tab out of the box.
- **Make it yours:** edit the category, invoice-status and project-status lists
  on the **Settings** tab.

## Works in
Microsoft Excel (desktop & 365) · Google Sheets · Apple Numbers · LibreOffice Calc
